How To Play

  • 🛒 Use buttons to move the basket.
  • 💎 Catch diamonds to score points.
  • 💣 Avoid bombs — 1 hit ends the game.
  • ❤️ Start with 3 lives; miss a diamond = lose 1.
  • 🔁 Catch 5 diamonds in a row to regain 1 life.
  • 🆙 Level up = +1 life. Game gets harder!
  • 🎁 Each level brings rewards & surprises.
  • 🎯 Stay sharp and catch smart!
